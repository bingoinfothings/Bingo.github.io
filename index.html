The Drop Pricing System

Be First. Get the Best Picks.

Every item starts fresh at $25 on Restock Day — with the widest selection available right at opening. As days go on, prices drop automatically across all remaining items until the next restock.

On select days, special event pricing may apply, with some items occasionally available for as low as $1.

D1

Restock Day — $25

Full fresh inventory available. Maximum choice and first access to all new arrivals.

D2

Day Two — $20

Prices adjust while new discoveries continue to appear on the floor.

D3

Day Three — $15

Strong value stage with evolving picks and high turnover of remaining items.

D4

Day Four — $10

Deep value opportunities as inventory becomes more limited.

D5

Day Five — $7

Ultra-value stage with remaining hidden gems.

D6

Day Six — $5

Final clearance pricing as the cycle nears reset.

D7

Day Seven — $3

Absolute lowest pricing before full restock refresh.
